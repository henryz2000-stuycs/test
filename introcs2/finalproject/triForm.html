Shows you the perimeter and area of any triangle.

Works as long as there are 3 sides to the triangle and it is a legit triangle.

Usage:

Enter the lengths of the three sides in the text fields, then click submit.
The computer will take these lengths, and calculate the perimeter and area of a triangle for you.

ProTips:

  1. Make sure to enter a legit triangle.
  2. Make sure to insert lengths for all 3 sides of the triangle
